How to hide your Posterous profile
I’m not claiming this makes your Posterous anonymous at all, but if you would like to hide your profile information from being displayed on your Posterous site/blog/whatever: Go to “Manage” Go to “Theme my site” Click on “Advanced” and name your new template Expand the editor using the link on the right Remove everything from and including <section id=“profile”> up to and including the relevant occurrence of Repeat for other sections if you want to remove contributor lists etc.
